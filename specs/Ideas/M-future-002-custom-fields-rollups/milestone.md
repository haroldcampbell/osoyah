## Summary
Plan a future milestone for customizable card fields and board-level rollups.

## Goal
Let users define custom properties on cards and compute rollups at the board level.

## Scope
In-scope:
- Define custom card fields (types, labels, validation).
- Display and edit custom fields on cards.
- Board-level rollups/aggregations over custom fields.
Out-of-scope:
- Cross-board rollups.
- Advanced analytics dashboards.
- Backend persistence or multi-user collaboration.
