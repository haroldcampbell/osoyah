# M-diff-002-Clarity-Trust: Trust Signals + Confidence

Conform to `docs/principles.md`.

## Summary
Expose trust signals and confidence rollups so teams can answer “Do I trust this view?” quickly without manual audits.

## Goal
Make trustworthiness and confidence explicit, explainable, and consistent across tasks, boards, and rollups.

## Scope
In-scope:
- Trust signals (stale, missing owner, broken link, needs review).
- Status confidence labels derived from trust signals.

Out-of-scope:
- Predictive forecasting or ML scoring.
- Real-time collaboration or presence.
