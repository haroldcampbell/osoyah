# M-diff-005-Context-Collaboration: Decision Timeline

Conform to `docs/principles.md`.

## Summary
Preserve rationale with decision notes attached to cards/boards, giving teams a lightweight decision timeline.

## Goal
Users can capture and retrieve decision context quickly without using external docs.

## Scope
In-scope:
- Decision notes with short rationale.
- Timeline UI for decisions on cards and boards.

Out-of-scope:
- Long-form documentation systems.
- Chat or presence features.
